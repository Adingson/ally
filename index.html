Reason

It was 2020 and the covid-19 pandemic had just started, I just graduated from high school and everyday I wake up with nothing to do than to just stare at my computer for the whole day. I was very skinny often staying up very late playing games all night and not waking up for lunch. as days goes by my patience was slowly getting worse and so was my confidence. My arms and legs were as thin as a stick and my health was worsening. I hated the way my body looked, scrawny looking along with having pimples, I didn't want to be insecure anymore, I wanted to be able to look at myself in the mirror and not be ashamed. Though my first 1-5 months were hard, I tried my best to be consistent, the gym journey was expensive and I didnt have much money on me. And the hardest part of it was the nutrition, I forced myself to eat as much as i can in order to grow. And now looking back, I can confidently say that I didn't have any regrets, I enjoyed every bit of it and hopefully will stay consistent.
